Introducing Reviews

You can now review your work and add comments into the text. Just select some text and either click Screenplay → Add Review to Selection or press ⌘R. There's also Review Mode, a full mode just for creating notes and comments. In Review Mode, a new review note will be created simply by selecting a region.

All review notes you add into the screenplay will be displayed in side bar.

Updated Privacy Policy

The Beat Privacy Policy has been updated. Policy itself hasn't changed but the wording is now more clear. Beat still respects the privacy of users: The app does not collect any information, files are stored on your personal device and one else has access to your work.
